1. Characteristics of the plant kingdom

Plants are eukaryotic multicellular organisms. Plant cells have chloroplasts, a cell wall and large vacuoles. Plants are autotrophic organisms: they make their own food through photosynthesis.

2. Plant nutrition and photosynthesis

Plants use water, carbon dioxide and sunlight to produce oxygen and organic substances. Photosynthesis takes place in the chloroplasts.

WATER + CARBON DIOXIDE + SUNLIGHT -> OXYGEN + ORGANIC SUBSTANCES
